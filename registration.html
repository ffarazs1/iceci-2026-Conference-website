IMPORTANT NOTES

  • UG/PG and Ph.D. Research Scholars will be considered as students.
  • Registration fee includes conference kit, access to all sessions, and lunch/tea during conference days.
  • IEEE members must provide valid IEEE membership details.
  • Students must upload valid institutional ID.
  • Each registration covers one accepted paper only.

PAYMENT INSTRUCTIONS

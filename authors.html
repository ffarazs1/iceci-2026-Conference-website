Paper Submission

Authors are invited to submit original, unpublished research papers that are not under review in any other conference or journal. All submitted papers will undergo a double-blind peer review process.

SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

  • Papers must be prepared in IEEE standard two-column format (A4 size).
  • Maximum page limit: 6 pages including figures, tables, and references.
  • Papers must be submitted in PDF format only .
  • Plagiarism must be below 20% as per IEEE guidelines.
  • All accepted papers will be published in IEEE Xplore Digital Library and indexed in Scopus.

The purpose of the 2nd IEEE International Conference on Electrical, Control and Instrumentation engineering (IEEE ICECIE’2025) is to bring together researchers, engineers and practitioners interested on Control and Systems , Energy and Environment , Industrial Informatics and Computational Intelligence and other aspects of control systems and electrical engineering.

VENUE

The conference will be held at the Department of Computer Science, Aligarh Muslim University (AMU), Aligarh, India — an institution of national importance (Seventh Schedule, Article 246 of the Indian Constitution).

About AMU: AMU is a premier academic institution, accredited by NAAC with an “A+” Grade (CGPA 3.35) and consistently ranked among India’s top universities. It secured #3 in India Today’s 2023 University Rankings , is placed among the Top 10 in NIRF 2021 , and is featured in the ARWU (Shanghai Rankings) and Times Higher Education World Rankings for its excellence in Life Sciences and Physical Sciences .

About the Department of Computer Science: Established in 1971 with the IBM1130 system, the department is one of India’s oldest and most pioneering in Computer Science and IT education. It became an independent department in 1988 and has consistently introduced cutting-edge programs, including Ph.D., MCA, M.Sc. in Cyber Security & Digital Forensics , and B.Sc. (Hons.) in Computer Science & Applications . Recognized by Times Higher Education, India Today, and NIRF , the department is equipped with state-of-the-art labs, smart classrooms, and modern infrastructure.

Graduates from the department are highly sought after by leading IT firms like TCS, IBM, Wipro, Infosys , and Accenture , with many securing placements before completing their final semester. Its strong academic and research ecosystem makes it a center of excellence in Computer Science education in India.

PLAGIARISM

Please note that all manuscripts submitted to the ICECI will be checked for plagiarism using turitin. Should the submitted paper contains a high level of similarity to previously published works, the manuscript will be withdrawn from the conference.

In addition, the authors will be checked if they have been prohibited from publishing with any or all IEEE publications using The IEEE Prohibited Authors List (PAL) database.
